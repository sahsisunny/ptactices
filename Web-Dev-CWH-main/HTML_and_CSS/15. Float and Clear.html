Computer

Computer is an electronic device that is designed to work with Information. It can store, retrieve, and process data. The first electronic digital computer was developed at the University of Pennsylvania in 1946. Since then, computer technology has rapidly developed, and it is now used in every field.

Fruits

Fruits are the sweet and pulpy part of a plant that contains seeds. Fruits are the most important source of vitamins and minerals. They are also a good source of fiber. Fruits are available in different colors, shapes, and sizes. They are eaten raw or cooked.

Vegetables

Vegetables are the edible parts of a plant that is used in cooking. They are rich in vitamins, minerals, and fiber. They are also low in calories. Vegetables are available in different colors, shapes, and sizes. They are eaten raw or cooked.
