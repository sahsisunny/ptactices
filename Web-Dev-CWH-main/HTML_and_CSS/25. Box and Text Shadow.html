White Hat Hacker

White hat hackers are the good guys. They are the ones who find security vulnerabilities in a system and report them to the company or organization that owns the system. They are also known as ethical hackers.

Black Hat Hacker

Black hat hackers are the bad guys. They are the ones who find security vulnerabilities in a system and exploit them for their own gain. They are also known as crackers.
